Over 90% of web experiences start on search engines such as Google, Bing, Yahoo, etc. At Ciel, we know that people usually find your brand first through a search engineBuilding a solid foundation in SEO and investing in it can lead to explosive growth. This initial interaction on a search engine also means exposure to a high-intent audience — these are people who specifically seek an answer, and it’s important that your brand provides an answer to their question.

SEO doesn’t need a big initial investment, and if done right, it can greatly benefit your business. Ciel has worked with many companies that have achieved YOY growth by strictly executing organic SEO.

Ciel provides end-to-end SEO services. These services include technical SEO, on-page SEO, off-page SEO, and more. Our experts create strong frameworks that lead to higher organic traffic, first-page keyword ranking, and more conversions. We understand the importance of reaching the right audience. Our methods make sure that when your brand first connects with them, it leaves a lasting impression and produces real outcomes.

SEO has significantly evolved over the years. Traditional methods of keyword stuffing and blackhat SEO won’t work in the long term. Search engine algorithms are very advanced now, so trying to trick them could hurt your business instead of helping it. If SEO is a key focus area, you’ll want to also think about Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) so you can future-proof your SEO and organic traffic efforts.
